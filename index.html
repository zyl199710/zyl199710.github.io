Hello. I am Yilong Zang (臧屹隆). Thank you for visiting!

I am now a second-year PhD student at PolyU SHTM. My supervisor is Dr. Hengyun Neil Li.

My research interests focus on the application of GNNs and LLMs to business survival prediction, as well as interesting and challenging hotel- and tourism-related tasks.

Prior to that, I was honored to work as a research assistant in the DSA group at the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI) under the supervision of Prof. Sebastian Vollmer.

I received my master’s degree from the National Engineering Research Center For Multimedia Software at Wuhan University in September 2023. During that time, I was supervised by Prof. Ruimin Hu and Prof. Zheng Wang as co-advisor. My master’s research task was graph-based fraud detection. I mainly analyzed new problems and patterns of fraudsters in graph data, and designed suitable GNN models to solve these problems. During my spare time, I like swimming, fitness, and badminton.
